ABOUT US

Hi! We are Job and Nikita (Kiet), creators and proud founders of Resu_mate.

Netherlands, 2025

Resu-mate started after we both fell into the trap of sneaky monthly payments when we thought we’d only have to pay once for a resume builder. We were frustrated with hidden fees and confusing contracts, and that’s when we realized—why not build our own resume tool?

Job’s interest in programming and Nikita’s passion for design came together, and we decided to turn our hobbies into something useful. We created Resu-mate to be simple, transparent, and affordable. No fine print, no hidden fees—just €5 for a whole year. We believe building your resume shouldn’t be a hassle or break the bank.

Think of us as your "mate" who’s here to help you create a professional resume with ease, for a small fee, no strings attached!
